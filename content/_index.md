# Where AI systems are grown.

**AI Liget** is a Budapest-based studio building production AI systems and the applications around them. We bring decades of enterprise experience — including time at **EY**, **bp**, and **Tesco** — and work end-to-end, from concept to production, for clients worldwide.

*Liget* /ˈli.ɡɛt/ is Hungarian for *grove*. Budapest's central park is called *Városliget* — the *city grove*. We chose the name because a grove is where careful things grow slowly, and that's how we think about building AI.
