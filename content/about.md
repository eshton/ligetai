## A grove in Budapest.

**Liget** /ˈli.ɡɛt/ is Hungarian for *grove* — a small wooded park. Budapest's central park is called *Városliget*, the *city grove*. We chose the name because a grove is where careful things grow slowly, and that's how we think about building AI: planted carefully, given time, and shaped to the ground it grows in.

## What we do.

AI Liget is a studio building production AI systems — and the web, backend, and mobile applications around them. We take projects from the first sketch to something running in your stack, and we don't outsource the hard parts.

The market is loud. A lot of shops pitch strategy and hand over slides; many others build fast and leave you with a demo that breaks on Monday morning. We'd rather do less and finish it.

## How we work.

- **Milestone-based engagements.** Scoped around outcomes, not hours. We aren't a vendor filling timesheets.
- **End-to-end delivery.** From concept to production — architecture, model work, apps, infrastructure, handover.
- **Boutique by choice.** We take on a few engagements at a time so each one gets full attention.
- **Careful by default.** We choose boring, reliable patterns where they fit — and use new tools where they earn it.

## Where we are.

Based in **Budapest, Hungary**. We work with clients worldwide.
